Paula Fraile Ruiz

The player

Paula is a 22 years old and 6'4ft tall basketball player born in Spain (Barcelona, 2002)

She is very athletic as well as versatile player who is able to play as a powerforward/center depending the team's necessities

Basketball Career

Paula has played in a few recognized Spain's bball teams as well as in D1 NCAA in the US.

Her basketball career has been the following:

  1. Club Basquet Olesa
    • From 2008 to 2013
  2. Joventut l'Hospitalet
    • From 2014 to 2016
  3. Femení Sant Adrià
    • From 2017 to 2018
  4. Segle XXI Club
    • From 2019 to 2020
  5. University of Miami WBB
    • From 2020 to 2022
